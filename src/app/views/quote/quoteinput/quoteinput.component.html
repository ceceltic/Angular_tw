What Is Two Wheeler Insurance?

Two wheeler insurance policy falls under the general insurance category in India. As per Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, two wheeler insurance is obligatory in the country. Bike insurance policies are designed to shield the owner of a vehicle from any unexpected or unforeseen incidents such as road accidents or any such. Online two wheeler insurance policy offers coverage to the rider or owner of a motorcycle against various unpredictable damages due to unforeseen circumstances like theft, disasters etc. Online bike insurance policy provides coverage for all types of two wheelers, be it a scooty, scooter, motorcycle or a sports bike regardless of its personal or commercial use. Two wheeler insurance in India is provided only by the insurance companies that are registered under IRDAI. Bike insurance is the ultimate solution to battle with the high costs of damages done to your motor cycle due to an accident. Two wheeler insurance online in India is a legal contract between the owner of a two wheeler and a two wheeler insurance company that assures of reimbursement in case of any damage or loss. The Sum Insured for two wheeler insurance is determined upon the IDV (Insured Declared Value) of your bike. You will have to pay a certain amount of money as a premium to enjoy the benefits of online two wheeler insurance policies in India. Bike insurance online policy can be purchased with effective additional covers. It’s advisable to compare two wheeler insurance online to get the best coverage for your bike.

Importance of Two Wheeler Insurance Policies

There are basically two types of two wheeler plans available to choose from – Third Party Two Wheeler Insurance and Comprehensive Two Wheeler Insurance. You will get the coverage depending upon the type of online two wheeler insurance plan you choose. Compare two wheeler insurance online to avail the best plan for your two wheeler at an affordable price. Third Party:Third party liability only plan for two wheeler is compulsory for every vehicle on road. The third party bike insurance online policy covers any loss or damage caused to a third party or their property. The policy pays for third party property damage upto Rs. 7.5 lacs. The only drawback of third party two wheeler insurance is that the policy does not provide any financial cover for the insured vehicle or its occupants. Comprehensive:Comprehensive bike insurance policy offers total coverage for your two wheeler. The policy takes care of legal liability and own damage as well. Comprehensive two wheeler insurance plan comes with extensive rider and flexible options and the coverage can be enhanced as per your requirements and preferences.

What Is Covered Under Two Wheeler Insurance Policy India?

Motorcycle insurance online has wide-ranging coverage on offer for both the vehicle and its rider. Relevant in this context, before you renew or buy two wheeler insurance online, remember that the cheapest bike insurance plans may not provide you with the type of coverage you are looking for. So it’s always wise to go for an affordable yet the best bike insurance online than opting for the cheapest priced two wheeler insurance for your bike or scooter to cut cost on your premium. Here’s what two wheeler insurance policies cover. Third Party Liability:In the event where a third party suffers injuries or death, or his/her property is damaged because of your vehicle, your motorcycle insurance will cover the financial liability. Natural Calamities:Online two wheeler insurance policies provide protection against natural calamities like earthquakes, fire, lightning, floods, rockslides, landslides, typhoons or storms, hurricanes, cyclones, frost, explosions, rain, self-ignitions, inundations etc. Manmade Calamities:Protection against manmade calamities such as a mishap or accident resulting from external factors like strikes, thefts, riots, vandalism, collision, burglary, malicious acts etc. Two wheeler insurance policies also cover the damages during transit via rail, road, air or water. Personal Accident Coverage:Riders of two wheelers are at a higher risk than any other vehicle drivers. Personal accident cover for two wheeler insurance provides compensation for damages suffered while riding, mounting or dismounting the bike.

Add-on Covers for Two Wheeler Insurance Policy in India

Add-on covers for two wheeler are basically extra coverage options that you purchase for a better financial protection to deal with accidents or other mishaps. Different add-ons for two wheeler come with different costs and benefits and could add a few thousand rupees to your basic bike insurance premium cost. Therefore, add-ons certainly impact the premium amount you pay. Two wheeler insurance company offers various add-on covers for the protection of your two wheeler. Here are some of the best add-on covers you can purchase with your two wheeler policy. Zero Depreciation:Your online comprehensive two wheeler Insurance policy will compensate for the repair costs only after the insurance company has deducted the depreciation amount of your bike’s damaged parts. But if you have zero depreciation add-on you can claim for the depreciated amount as well. Thus you will receive the whole value irrespective of the depreciation amount. Pillion Rider Add-on Cover:This add-on offers coverage for the pillion rider - the person sitting behind your two wheeler. In case the co-passenger is injured, this additional personal accident cover for the pillion rider will provide compensation. Note that this add-on is totally separate from a personal accident cover for owner-driver. Return to Invoice Cover:This add-on cover comes into rescue in case your bike suffers damage that is beyond repairs. If you have this add-on, the bike insurance company will pay the entire market value of your bike when you file a claim. The amount that you will be paid will have nothing to do with depreciation. Roadside Assistance Cover:If your two wheeler is broken down or damaged in the middle of the road, you can simply call your two wheeler insurance company for assistance. If you have this add-on, your insurance provider will send a mechanic for the repairing of your vehicle on the spot. Your insurer will send a towing car to take your two wheeler to the garage if the damage can't be repaired on the spot.
